About This Map

Purpose

The Sprouse Hollow Explorer is an interactive map centered on 271 Sprouse Hollow Lane, Craigsville, Augusta County, Virginia. It layers live parcel data from Augusta County GIS over historical points of interest drawn from local records and oral history.

Data Sources

  • Parcel & Property Data: Augusta County GIS Public REST Service — gis.co.augusta.va.us
  • Property Assessment Records: Augusta County Assessor via gis.vgsi.com/augustava
  • Historical Iron Furnaces: Virginia Iron Furnaces Project & Virginia Dept. of Historic Resources
  • Place Names & Topo: USGS Geographic Names Information System (GNIS) & National Map
  • Historical & Genealogical Records: Augusta County Deed Books, Wills, Census Records, local oral tradition
  • Satellite Imagery: Esri World Imagery (Esri, Maxar, Earthstar Geographics)
  • Street Map: © OpenStreetMap contributors, CC-BY-SA

Disclaimers

  • Parcel data is provided by Augusta County GIS and is for informational purposes only. Not a survey. Not legally authoritative.
  • Some historical information is based on oral tradition and local genealogical records. Primary documentary sources may be limited.
  • GPS accuracy varies by device and environment. The Simulate Location feature is for remote exploration only.
  • Historical POI coordinates are approximate and intended for general reference.
